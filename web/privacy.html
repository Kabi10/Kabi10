Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

Agrimarket collects the following information to provide our agricultural marketplace service:

  • Phone number: Used for account verification via OTP and as your primary identifier.
  • Name and location: Provided by you to identify yourself to other users on the platform.
  • Listing data: Crop type, quantity, pricing, images, and descriptions you provide when creating listings.
  • Transaction data: Records of purchases and sales made through the platform.
  • Messages: Communications between buyers and farmers through our in-app messaging.
  • Device information: Basic device type and OS version for app compatibility and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and maintain your account
  • To display your listings to potential buyers
  • To facilitate communication between buyers and farmers
  • To process and track transactions
  • To send notifications about orders and messages
  • To improve the app and fix issues

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited information only as follows:

  • Other users: Your name, location, and listing details are visible to other users. Your phone number is shared only with users you transact with.
  • Service providers: We use Twilio for SMS verification and Supabase for database hosting. These providers process data only on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by Sri Lankan law.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure authentication tokens. The app stores some data locally on your device for offline access.

5. Your Rights

  • You can update your profile information at any time in the app.
  • You can delete your account, which will deactivate your profile and listings.
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Transaction records are kept for legal compliance purposes. You can request account deletion at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Agrimarket is not intended for use by children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at admin@agrimarket.lk.
