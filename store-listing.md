## Full Description

### English
Agrimarket connects farmers directly to buyers across Sri Lanka. Buy fresh produce at fair prices. Sell your harvest without middlemen.

**For Farmers:**
- List your crops with photos, quantity, and pricing
- Receive orders directly from buyers
- Chat with interested buyers
- Track your sales and earnings

**For Buyers:**
- Browse fresh produce from local farmers
- Filter by crop type, location, and price
- Place orders and arrange pickup
- Rate and review farmers

**Key Features:**
- Works offline with poor signal
- Available in Tamil, English, and Sinhala
- Real-time chat between buyers and farmers
- Market price tracking for informed decisions
- Secure OTP-based login
